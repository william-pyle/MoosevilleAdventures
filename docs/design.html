Core Gameplay Loop

🧭 Explore
→
⛏️ Gather Resources
→
🌱 Farm & Craft
→
🏗️ Build & Upgrade
→
📈 Town Grows

Design intent: Explorer, farmer, and city-builder are not separate modes — they are one interconnected loop. Solo effort can keep the town alive, but community infrastructure is required to grow it efficiently.
🪙

The Mucks Economy

💰

Currency: Mucks

Carved wooden coins with antler stamps and seasonal markings. The concept was learned from the Sacred Documents and redesigned in Moosian form.

Earn Sources
  • City work orders and contracts
  • Main and side quest rewards
  • Festival event prizes
  • Selling crafted specialty goods
  • Exploration deliveries and mapped route reports
🛒

Spend Sources

Purchasable (Non-Essential)
  • Clothing and style sets
  • Home upgrades and décor
  • Cosmetic station services
  • Optional efficiency tools
  • Specialty crafted foods and celebration goods
Free Forever: Moose Water · Basic food staples
No citizen loses identity or health due to poverty.

Production Throughput Model

🤲

Manual Production

  • Always available — no unlock required
  • Low throughput, stamina-heavy
  • Solo effort can keep the town alive
Design intent: survival through effort
⚙️

Industrial Production

  • Requires business unlock + staffed role
  • High throughput, maintenance cost in mucks and fuel
  • Community infrastructure needed to grow efficiently
Design intent: expansion through infrastructure

Quest Campaigns → Systems Unlocks

🌫️

Campaign I: The Fading Glow Crisis

  • System unlock: Water diagnostics board
  • Risk mechanic: Regression meter for impacted citizens
  • Win condition: Identify spring diversion source
  • Timeline: Year 4–5 post-founding
💚

Campaign II: The Green Water Expedition

  • System unlock: Enrichment Station
  • Resource unlock: Moose Water supply management
  • Win condition: Restore affected Moosians and stabilize water
🤝

Campaign III: Strangers from the Far Side

  • System unlock: Integration district and diplomacy tracks
  • Reward unlock: New styles, dyes, craft variants
  • Win condition: Successful aid and optional citizenship integration
🌄

Campaign IV: The Wide Valley Choice

  • System unlock: Repeatable recruitment expeditions
  • Ethical lock: Voluntary join-or-return choice required
  • Win condition: Recruit by consent and maintain town trust

Reward Design Rules

  • Clothing and upgrades can be purchased with mucks or granted as quest rewards
  • Main campaigns should always award one permanent town system unlock
  • Festival rewards should rotate between cosmetics, recipes, and civic buffs
  • Teaching milestones should reward player rank advancements and NPC certification tools
Two-axis progression model: personal mastery + community mastery. Player levels through exploration and quests; community levels through production infrastructure and NPC proficiency.
