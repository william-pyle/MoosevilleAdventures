The Core Concept

Mooseville Adventures is an Unreal Engine 5 open-world game that blends exploration, farming, and city-building in a sealed 20-square-mile valley. Twenty years ago, a meteor struck the eastern peaks — sealing the pass out of the valley and scattering sky-stone shards into a mountain spring below.

The moose who drank from that spring were transformed. Over generations they became bipedal, intelligent, and deeply curious. Now they build Mooseville: their first civilization — learning to farm, trade, govern, and care for one another while navigating the tension between ancient instinct and new intelligence.

"Mooseville exists because Moosians chose to care for one another while learning a world they did not ask to inherit."

Core Design Pillars

🔄

One Continuous Loop

Explorer, farmer, and city-builder are not separate modes — they are one interconnected gameplay loop. Exploration feeds resources. Resources feed farming. Farming feeds construction. Construction grows the town and opens new exploration.

🏛️

Contribution Drives Progress

Advancement comes through civic contribution: mucks earned, buildings raised, citizens trained. Solo effort can keep the town alive, but community infrastructure is required to grow it efficiently.

👔

Jobs Are Identity

Each citizen role carries a title, daily routine, outfit, and social affinity. NPCs are not generic workers — they are Moosians with personality, and their proficiency level shapes what the whole town can do.

💧

Essentials Are Free

Moose Water and basic food are always free. No citizen loses their mind or body due to poverty. The economy of mucks governs non-essentials, cosmetics, and specialty goods — never survival.

🦌

Always Moosian, Never Human

Every imported idea from the Sacred Documents was recast in Moosian form. Wide hoof-friendly rows in farming. Antler arches in architecture. Mane houses instead of barber shops. Useful idea, Moosian design.

🤝

Consent in Every Quest

Recruitment is invitation-based, never forced. Wild moose offered Moose Water are given a clear choice: join Mooseville or return to the wild. Both choices are honored. The town grows by trust, not conquest.
