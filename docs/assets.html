Production · Assets

Asset Catalog

The complete 3D asset planning brief: 12 businesses, 12 core infrastructure buildings, expansion structures, costume sets, props, and the full resource chain pipeline — all with moose-themed visual direction.

Tier System: A Town-critical, first playable loop  |  B Growth assets, economy and role progression  |  C Social depth and visual variety  |  D Late-game and narrative arcs

Global Building Style Rules

Primary Materials
  • Timber, stone, brick, clay tile
  • Iron fittings, woven reed panels
Architectural Language
  • Antler arches and beam carvings
  • Hoof-friendly ramps (no steps where possible)
  • Carved beam ends and tine motifs
  • Glowing green vial lantern accents
Scale & Color
  • Medium village scale
  • Doors and walkways oversized for broad-shouldered Moosians
  • Cedar brown, moss green, river slate, clay red, warm cream
Construction Rules
  • Visible production spaces reflect each building's function
  • Modular design — upgrades layer over base structures
  • Antler-safe door clearance on all entries

Building Briefs — Key Design Notes

🌱

BIZ-01 · Antler Almanac Co-op

A
Moose Theme

Roof ridge shaped like crossed antlers; weather vane is a carved moose head.

Key Exterior

Open front canopy for seed stalls, chalk board wall, hanging planting charts.

Signature Props

Seed barrels, weather rods, moon-phase board, carved furrow markers.

🔨

BIZ-02 · Rack and Rafter Works

A
Moose Theme

Massive beam trusses carved to resemble tine branching.

Key Exterior

Wide yard with stacked logs, crane gantry, half-built frame display.

Signature Props

Timber saw horses, peg bins, blueprint rolls, load harness rack.

💧

BIZ-12 · Green Vial Commons

A
Moose Theme

Glowing green vials integrated into every architectural detail — sills, arches, lanterns.

Key Exterior

Open-front distribution counter, purity gauge displays, queue lanes for citizens.

Special Rule

No sales transactions allowed at this building — free distribution only, by civic law.

📦

BIZ-07 · Muck and Measure Market Hall

A
Moose Theme

Large carved muck coin medallion over entry arch.

Key Exterior

Arcaded market front, stall bays, price board columns.

Signature Props

Scale sets, coin trays, crate stacks, rotating deal placards.
