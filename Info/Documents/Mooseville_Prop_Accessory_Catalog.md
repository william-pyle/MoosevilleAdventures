# Mooseville Prop and Accessory Creation Catalog

Purpose: This file defines wearable accessories, hand props, station tools, and environment props for Mooseville.

Primary use:
- Character accessories that match role costumes.
- Building props that match business and infrastructure function.
- Modular object library for AI 3D generation.

## Global Art and Modeling Direction

- Visual language: handcrafted, practical, warm, and unmistakably moose-themed.
- Material focus: carved wood, stitched cloth, brushed iron, rope, leather, ceramic, glass.
- Shape language: rounded corners, antler arcs, hoof-safe handles, large readable silhouettes.
- Symbol system: antler crest, hoof mark, pinecone seal, green vial emblem, muck coin stamp.

## Prop Tiering and Rarity

Common:
- Everyday tools and utility objects used by most citizens.
- Lower ornamentation, high readability, simple material stack.

Uncommon:
- Profession-specific accessories and improved versions of common tools.
- Distinct role markings and enhanced utility detail.

Rare:
- Festival items, specialist tools, and milestone-unlock props.
- Strong visual identity and layered materials.

Legendary:
- Story arc rewards tied to campaign milestones and leadership ranks.
- Unique silhouette, custom insignia, and lore-linked ornament.

## Technical Generation Specifications

Per prop category:
- Small props: 500 to 2,500 tris
- Medium props: 2,500 to 8,000 tris
- Large station props: 8,000 to 25,000 tris

Texture guidance:
- 2K base for hero props
- 1K base for common props
- Shared trim sheets for wood, cloth, iron, and rope

Rigging guidance:
- Hand props should include grip points for both forelimb animation sets.
- Wearables should use modular sockets on belt, chest, shoulder, and back.

LOD guidance:
- LOD0 hero, LOD1 gameplay, LOD2 crowd/background.
- Keep silhouette consistency across all LOD levels.

## Naming Convention for Assets

Prefix by category:
- ACC_ for wearable accessories
- HND_ for hand props
- STN_ for station props
- ENV_ for environment packs

Examples:
- ACC_Grower_SeedSatchel_A
- HND_Builder_PegMallet_A
- STN_Archive_MapDrawer_A
- ENV_Bakehouse_ExteriorPack_A

## Delivery Checklist for Each Prop Pack

- LODs and pivot points validated
- Naming follows convention
