# Mooseville Building Creation Catalog

Purpose: This file is a 3D asset planning brief for Mooseville. It lists core businesses and town infrastructure with moose-themed visual direction and function-specific requirements.

Use notes for AI 3D generation:
- Keep all buildings stylized rustic-fantasy with practical town function.
- Prioritize antler-safe doors, wide entries, and curved corners.
- Include signage and motifs that are moose-themed, never human copy.
- Keep models modular so upgrades can be layered over base structures.

## Global Style Rules

- Primary materials: timber, stone, brick, clay tile, iron fittings, woven reed panels.
- Architectural language: antler arches, hoof-friendly ramps, carved beam ends, glowing green vial lantern accents.
- Scale: medium village scale; doors and walkways oversized for broad-shouldered Moosians.
- Color baseline: cedar brown, moss green, river slate, clay red, warm cream.
- Utility design: visible production spaces should reflect each building's function.

## Business Buildings (12 Required)

Moose Theme: Roof ridge shaped like crossed antlers; weather vane is a carved moose head.
Exterior Requirements: Open front canopy for seed stalls, chalk board wall, hanging planting charts.
Interior Zones: Planning desk, seed vault, soil sample shelf, seasonal map table.
Signature Props: Seed barrels, weather rods, moon-phase board, carved furrow markers.

## Core Town Infrastructure Buildings

### A. Town Hall of Tines
Function: Governance, civic decisions, job assignment office.
Design Notes: Broad steps, antler crown roofline, public speaking balcony.
Needed Spaces: Council chamber, records office, assignment desk.

### B. Commons Kitchen and Free Table
Function: Free staple meals for all citizens.
Design Notes: Large central hearth, long communal benches, covered serving lane.
Needed Spaces: Bulk pantry, prep line, dish wash zone, storage cellar.

### C. Sawmill Yard
Function: Bulk conversion of logs to boards and beams.
Design Notes: Waterwheel or gear drive, conveyor lanes, timber drying racks.
Needed Spaces: Intake yard, saw line, board stack bays, maintenance pit.

### D. Quarry Works
Function: Rock extraction and stone block shaping.
Design Notes: Terraced pit edge, crane posts, stone dust channels.
Needed Spaces: Cut zone, shaping benches, block staging area.

### E. Brick Kiln Complex
Function: Clay firing into bricks and ceramic utility parts.
Design Notes: Multi-kiln chimneys, heat vents, clay settling troughs.
Needed Spaces: Clay prep yard, kiln hall, cooling racks.

### F. Smelter and Forge Hall
Function: Iron refining, nails and hardware production.
Design Notes: Tall smoke stacks, blast furnace core, forge glow windows.
Needed Spaces: Ore intake, furnace floor, hammer stations, quench troughs.

### G. Granary and Seed Vault
Function: Food reserve and protected seed storage.
Design Notes: Raised floor structure, rodent-proof stone base, dry airflow vents.
Needed Spaces: Grain bins, seed lockers, weighing station.

### H. Barn and Pack Stable
Function: Tool carts, pack rigs, utility harness storage.
Design Notes: Extra-wide doors, side loading ramp, weatherproof canopy.
Needed Spaces: Harness wall, wagon bay, repair corner.

### I. Reservoir and Irrigation Gatehouse
Function: Water storage and farm channel control.
Design Notes: Stone basin walls, sluice gates, elevated control walkway.
Needed Spaces: Gate controls, flow meter station, emergency spill route.

### J. Roadworks Depot
Function: Path paving, bridge maintenance, signpost management.
Design Notes: Paver stacks, tar pitch vats, marker totem workshop.
Needed Spaces: Tool lockup, material bay, route planning board.

### K. Watchtower and Storm Bell
Function: Hazard warning, fire watch, weather alerts.
Design Notes: Tall timber tower, bell frame, signal lantern mast.
Needed Spaces: Lookout platform, bell controls, alert storage.

### L. Clinic and Recovery Lodge
Function: Rest and recovery for injuries and regression episodes.
Design Notes: Quiet enclosed courtyard, soft-lit rooms, clean water line.
Needed Spaces: Intake room, recovery cots, treatment prep station.
